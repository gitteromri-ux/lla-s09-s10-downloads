## E · Transitions (copy-paste techniques, no plugins needed)

| # | Transition | How (editor recipe) |
|---|---|---|
| T1 | **Light-wipe match** | Cut on the lamp/sun flare crossing frame center; 2-frame white lift between shots |
| T2 | **Speed-ramp whip** | Last 6 frames of outgoing shot ramp 100→400%, first 4 of incoming 400→100% |
| T3 | **Match-cut geometry** | Circle→circle (ice bath overhead → supplement capsule → watch face) |
| T4 | **Data dissolve** | Outgoing shot pixelates into navy data particles → particles assemble incoming card (After Effects: CC Ball Action 8f) |
| T5 | **Hard cut on beat** | No effect. The Atelier weapon. Use on every VO clause landing |
| T6 | **Depth pull** | Rack focus out on outgoing → rack in on incoming (pairs A2↔C3) |

## F · Grade
- Humans: Kodak Portra emulation — highlights +10 Y / +5 R, shadows −5 B, skin protected, +8 contrast, 4% grain (per brief)
- Expensive-world: −10 saturation, +5 B shadows, steel-blue LUT
- Graphics/data: pure brand navy `#0E1F48` fields, bone `#F5F2EA` type, blue `#006EFF` + violet/green accents only
