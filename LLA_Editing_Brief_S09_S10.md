## Global spec (applies to both films)

### Capture / grade
- 4K horizontal master → two virtual 9:16 crops in post: **WIDE (chest-up)** and **TIGHT (eyes-to-mouth)**.
- Grade: highlights **+10 Y / +5 R**, shadows **–5 B**, skin protected via HSL qualifier, **+8 contrast**, optional **4% film grain**.
- Aperture f/2.8 · 50mm-equiv · eyeline **just off-camera**, not to lens · key light camera-left 45°, slightly above eye · no fill · **~3200 K** · background 1.5–2 stops under face.

### Audio
- Lav under wardrobe · **no music, zero** · silence is the aesthetic · light compression only · master **-14 LUFS**.

### Captions (mandatory spec)
- **Inter SemiBold** (or SF Pro Display Semibold), **48–52 px** on 1080×1920.
- White `#FFFFFF` · shadow `x:0 y:2 blur:8 opacity:0.6 #000000` · **no stroke** · center · **Y 60–65%** from top (above Shorts UI) · sentence case with real punctuation · 4–7 words per line, max 2 lines · **swap exactly on each cut** · real italics for emphasis / foreign phrases.
- **Banned fonts:** Arial, Helvetica, Times, any system default.

### Logo / lockup
- File: `assets/lla-logo-transparent@2x.png` (or `lla-logo-exact-transparent@2x.png`).
- Composition: DNA/orbital mark → **LONGEVITY LIFE ACADEMY** (uppercase, Codec Pro) → **by eTeacher Group**.
- **Always on navy `#0E1F48`.** Never recolor. Never remove "by eTeacher Group." Never wordmark-only in video.

### Color anchors
`#0E1F48` navy · `#081634` deep · `#006EFF` blue · `#F5F2EA` bone · `#161A18` ink · navy gradient `linear-gradient(180deg, #0E1F48 0%, #081634 100%)`

### Brand rails (auto-reject if violated)
- **No fear-of-death framing.** Gain-frame only: presence, strength, good years.
- **Banned verbs:** unlock, supercharge, hack, 10x, biohack.
- **No exclamation points in VO. No emoji anywhere.**
- Cite data plainly with named sources.
